About OMF Geometry

A place where the geometric tattoo community lives outside of Instagram's algorithm.

@omfgeometry started the way most things worth doing start — not as a business idea, but as something that just needed to exist. Geometric tattooing has its own world: its own artists, its own standards, its own obsession with precision. But that world was scattered across Instagram feeds, buried in hashtags, disappearing into an algorithm that doesn't care whether the work is good — only whether it's recent.

This is the fix for that. A permanent directory of the best geometric tattoo artists in the world. A place you can actually browse, search by location, find someone whose work stops you. Not curated by an AI, not ranked by followers. Just hand-picked work that meets a simple standard: it has to be exceptional.

What we are

OMF Geometry is an independent platform run by @weschetattoo — a geometric tattoo artist with 12 years in the craft. Every artist in the directory was personally selected. Every post on the Instagram page is a repost of real work by real artists, with their permission and full credit.

There are no ads. No paid placements. No "submit your artist for $99." The community is the product — and you don't charge for a community to exist.

What we're building

The directory is the foundation. The Instagram page — @omfgeometry — is where the work surfaces daily. The blog is where we go deeper: technique, history, the artists themselves. And eventually, real-world presence — conventions, collaborations, the kind of things that only happen when a community has somewhere to gather.

If you're a geometric tattoo artist and you want to be part of it, submit your work. If you know someone who should be in the directory, send them our way.

— @omfgeometry
